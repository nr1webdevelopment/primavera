About Primavera

Factory-Direct Beauty from Source to Salon

Primavera is a LATAM nail salon franchise built on one core advantage: direct access to an FDA-approved nail polish factory in Guangzhou, China. This means 40–60% lower product costs than competitors who source through distributors.

We pair factory-direct pricing with a 1,500+ color portfolio — including plant-based bio formulas — and a proven franchise model built for the Mexican and broader LATAM market.
